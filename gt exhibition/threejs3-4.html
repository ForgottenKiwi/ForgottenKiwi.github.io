Basic Movement

This was my first main attempt for making a movement system that wasn't generated by chatgpt. It has a lot of issues like no acceleration, you can't look up or down (for some reason that requires a painful amount of math), you can't jump/no gravity... ok so actually it has a lot of issues. But, it is just a first attempt and will be my basis for all future movement systems.

Controls: WASD for movement + left/right arrows for rotating
